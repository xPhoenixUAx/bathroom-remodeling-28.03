Effective date: March 29, 2026

What cookies are

Cookies are small data files stored on a device when a website is visited. They can help remember settings, support analytics, and improve general site performance.

How cookies may be used

BathScope Guide may use cookies or similar technologies to understand site usage, maintain basic preferences, improve navigation, and measure which content homeowners find most useful.

Performance and analytics

Analytics tools may collect information such as pages viewed, device type, general location, traffic source, and interaction patterns to help improve the website experience.

Advertising-related technologies

Depending on how the platform is configured, cookies may also support campaign measurement, traffic attribution, and broader marketing performance reporting.

Managing cookie preferences

Most browsers allow cookies to be accepted, limited, or deleted. Restricting cookies may change how some parts of the site perform or how preferences are stored between visits.

Third-party services

Some website tools may be provided by third parties that set or read cookies according to their own privacy and cookie practices.

Updates to this policy

Cookie practices may change over time as the platform evolves. This page may be updated to reflect operational, legal, or technology changes.

Questions
