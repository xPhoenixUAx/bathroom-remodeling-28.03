Effective date: March 28, 2026

Information collected

BathScope Guide may collect information that homeowners submit through forms, including name, email address, phone number, ZIP code, project interests, and any details voluntarily entered into message fields.

How information is used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, improve site experience, understand service interests, and help identify independent local provider categories relevant to a submitted request.

Sharing with third parties

When a homeowner requests local matching, information may be shared with independent service providers or technology partners involved in routing that request.

The site may use cookies or similar technologies for performance, analytics, and website improvement. Browser settings can affect how cookies are accepted or rejected.

Information may be retained for operational, legal, security, and recordkeeping purposes for a period considered reasonably necessary.

Your choices

Homeowners may contact the platform to ask about access, correction, or deletion requests, subject to applicable legal obligations and operational requirements.

State-specific rights

Depending on state law, residents may have additional rights relating to personal information. Requests can be sent through the contact page or the email listed below.
