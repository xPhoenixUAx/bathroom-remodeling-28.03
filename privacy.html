Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

BathScope Guide ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy.

BathScope Guide is an independent platform created to help homeowners explore bathroom remodeling options, understand upgrade paths, and connect with independent local contractors. The website provides informational content and referral support but does not perform renovation services directly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with the Service.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a contact or inquiry form
  • Request to be connected with a contractor
  • Subscribe to updates or communications
  • Communicate with us directly

This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • General location (city, ZIP code)
  • Description of your remodeling interests or project goals
  • Any additional information you choose to share

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access the website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Navigation behavior and referring URLs

This data is used in aggregated or anonymized form whenever possible.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and understand how users interact with the website.

For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Operate and improve the website experience
  • Respond to inquiries and requests
  • Facilitate connections with independent third-party contractors
  • Provide relevant remodeling insights and educational content
  • Analyze usage trends and optimize performance
  • Communicate updates (where permitted)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information.

If you request assistance or submit a project inquiry, your information may be shared with independent contractors so they can contact you regarding your request.

These contractors operate independently and are responsible for their own services, communications, and data practices.

4.2 With Service Providers

These partners are required to handle data in accordance with applicable privacy standards.

4.3 Legal Obligations

We may disclose information when required to:

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring, information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures designed to protect your information, including:
