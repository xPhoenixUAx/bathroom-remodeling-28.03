Planning Considerations

Questions that often shape the final service choice.

  • How much clearance is available for doors, vanities, and entry?
  • Will the room need stronger slip resistance or seated bathing?
  • Is the priority a visual refresh or a layout shift?
  • Which surfaces will be easiest to maintain over time?
